Conversation classes

Ready to break through the language barrier? If you’ve already mastered the basics of Dutch and want to become more confident in real-world conversations, our Dutch Conversation Classes for Intermediate Learners are designed just for you!

Take Your Dutch to the Next Level

  • Boost Your Fluency: Our classes focus on practical, everyday conversations that will help you speak Dutch with confidence in real-life situations. No more memorizing grammar rules—just lots of talking!
  • Personalized Attention: Small class sizes mean you'll get plenty of speaking time and personalized feedback from our experienced instructors, all native Dutch speakers.
  • Advanced Vocabulary: Expand your vocabulary to talk about more complex subjects like work, studies, and even expressing your feelings or discussing abstract ideas.
  • Focus on Pronunciation and Idiomatic Phrases: I will help you refine your pronunciation, so you sound more fluent, and introduce you to common Dutch idioms and expressions to make your speech more authentic.
  • Real-Life Situations: Whether you’re making small talk with locals or engaging in more formal conversations, we’ll prepare you for any social setting.
  • Cultural Insights: Gain an understanding of Dutch customs, traditions, and etiquette, making your experience even richer.
